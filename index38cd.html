What is Telegram Open Network?

TON is a global ecosystem based on blockchain and Telegram messenger. It will include a full-scale system of services for payment processing, data storage, app development, and business optimization. All services within the ecosystem will interact as parts of a unified anonymous and secure framework.

What is Gram?

Gram is a token that acts as the internal currency of Telegram Open Network. It will be used for payments and distributed during the ICO.

Which information do I need to provide during registration?

You are not required to submit your ID or passport number, pass a verification, or perform any other actions that would limit or violate your privacy as an investor. You will only need to provide a set of standard registration data.

Who can invest into the project and buy Gram tokens?

Only those individuals who have reached the age of maturity in their country of residence can register on the website, use its features, and make investments in the project

Can I register multiple accounts?

No. Multiple accounts are forbidden; any violation of this rule, once discovered, will lead to all your accounts being blocked without a possibility of restoring them.

Are my personal data protected?

The project uses the latest cryptographic protocols that virtually eliminates any risk of our clients’ data getting stolen. Thanks to the the SSL certificate, our users can be sure that all their data are transmitted and processed securely.

Which payment processing systems are used by the project?

It is possible to perform transactions using fiat-based systems Perfect Money, Payeer, and NixMoney, as well as pay in Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Dogecoin, Litecoin.

Is it possible to receive automatic payouts straight into a wallet?

Automatic payouts are very convenient, but they present a serious security risk and can result in theft. In order to provide maximum protection to our investors, we carry out instant payments: in order to make a withdrawal, you need to create a request in your user area.

Can I withdraw my investment at any time?

Unfortunately, it is not possible to cancel an investment. Once you create a deposit, the funds are sent to the general project fund and allocated to finance the development of Telegram Open Network.

Is there a withdrawal fee?

We do not charge any fees on deposit or withdrawal operations; however, your chosen payment processing system can have its own fees.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes, we do reward our members for attracting new users, with the referral reward levels set at 10%-5%-3%-2%-1%.

How can I attract new users?

In order to attract new members into your referral network, you will need your personal referral link, which you fill find on your dashboard after registering.

What is a bounty program?

The Telegram Open Network bounty program offers you an extra opportunity to increase your investment profits. By making reposts in social media, leaving comments about our platform, filming YouTube video reviews and so on, you can receive a 1-2% bonus on your investment.

I’ve lost my password. Can I restore it?

Yes, you can restore your account by using the Restore Password form.

I haven’t found an answer to my question.

If you cannot find what you are looking for on the project website, please contact our support team.
